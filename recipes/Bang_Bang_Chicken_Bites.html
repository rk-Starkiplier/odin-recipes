Bang Bang Chicken Bites

Description

These bang bang chicken bites are spicy, crispy little chicken nuggets, air fried or baked, then tossed in a quick chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces frozen crispy chicken nuggets
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha hot sauce, plus more if desired
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 300 degrees F (193 degrees C)
  2. Add nuggets in a single layer to the air fryer basket, and fry until hot and crisp, about 10 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through cooking time.
  3. Meanwhile, stir mayonnaise, chili sauce, and hot sauce together in a large bowl until well blended.
