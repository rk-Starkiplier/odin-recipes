Melon Cucumber Gazpacho

Description

This melon cucumber gazpacho is a fruity blend of pureed and chopped fresh produce, with a mint-yogurt topping. Serve this cold soup as an appetizer for a summer backyard cookout, brunch, or a light and refreshing snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups seeded cubed watermelon, 1-inch cubes
  • 1 cup diced unpeeled English cucumber
  • 1 cup diced, seeded cantaloupe
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 small lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh tarragon
  • 1/3 cup plain reduced-fat Greek yogurt, such as FageÂ® 2%
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh mint

Steps

  1. Add watermelon to a blender or food processor, and pulse just until smooth but not completely liquified.
  2. Combine watermelon puree, cucumber, cantaloupe, salt, lemon juice, and tarragon in a bowl.
  3. Cover and refrigerate until fully chilled, at least 2 hours.
  4. Combine yogurt, basil, and mint in a small bowl. Pour gazpacho into cups or small bowls, top with a dollop of the yogurt mixture, and serve.
